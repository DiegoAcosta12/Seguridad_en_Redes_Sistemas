DESCRIPCION:
Can you convert the number 42 (base 10) to binary (base 2)?

SOLUCION:
Convertimos el número **42 en base 10** a **base 2**:

- 42 ÷ 2 = 21, residuo 0.

- 21 ÷ 2 = 10, residuo 1.

- 10 ÷ 2 = 5, residuo 0.

- 5 ÷ 2 = 2, residuo 1.

- 2 ÷ 2 = 1, residuo 0.

- 1 ÷ 2 = 0, residuo 1.
